F1 DRIVING EXPERIENCE

WHERE FANS ENJOY RACING!



F1 fans can enroll in the F1 Driving Experience to have the experience to learn physical training, learn the race track, practice with a racing simulator, race with a professional trainer on track, and finally be able to drive an F1 car with 5 laps.



RACING PREPERATION



Learn Physical Training

Take part in different required workouts to train as an F1 driver.



Learn the Race Track

Learn from professionals to understand the racing lines, braking points, and speeds.



Practice with a Racing Simulator

Practice with our enhanced racing simulator that takes you to close to life racing.



Race with a Professional on Track

Race with a Professional to teach you how to understand the levels of g-forces



Drive an F1 Williams Renault Car

Training will prepare to immerse yourself in a 2013 F1 Williams Renault Car.

Safety Information

We recommend always driving on a dry day rather than on a wet day, due to the risks of drivers who are not experienced driving in the rain. If the rain conditions are harsher on a day you are scheduled for the F1 Driving Experience, it may be rescheduled.
